          About

          Welcome to the G12 Learning Materials Organizer, a web-based platform developed as part of a high school research project.
          This website was created by student researchers to help organize, access, and share learning materials for Grade 12 subjects in a more intuitive and centralized way.
          Unlike traditional storage tools such as Google Drive or Classroom, this platform was designed specifically for students, featuring organized subject folders, community-uploaded notes, and features like integrated tests, AI-assisted learning, and a Pomodoro timer.
          If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions for improvement, please feel free to reach out to the research team.
